Questions

When should I arrive?

The wedding starts at 5:00 P.M. Guests can begin arriving 30 minutes prior! Any late arrivals can quietly sit in the back. If the ceremony has begun and the wedding party is still walking out, please allow them to finish before sitting down!

Ceremony and Reception?

The ceremony will take place outside unless there is expected rain. Please plan accordingly as it will be May and could potentially be hot. Hand fans will be provided on guests' chairs! The reception will be indoors with AC, Yay!

Dress Code?

We ask you to dress business casual, semi-formal, or formal. Whatever suits you! Please save the white for the bride.

Alcohol?

There will be no alcohol at the wedding. We want everyone to remember the day.

Food?

We will be having a food truck! This food truck will serve a variety of burgers with food allergies or restrictions in mind! We also will have a side bar, salad bar, and fruit bar! We hope there will be plenty of options for everyone to enjoy!

Can I take pictures?

While we appreciate you wanting to capture our special moment, we ask that you do not take pictures during the ceremony! Feel free to take some before or after! We want everyone to be present in the moment with no distractions! Take as many photos during the reception as you like!
We also ask that no photos be posted until the couple can post from that day! After that, feel free to post and use #MZSledgeWedding so we can view them!
There will also be QR codes located on the tables at the reception. You can use those to upload your photos as well.

Can I bring a plus one?

The invitation had the names of anyone who was invited to the wedding including whether or not a plus one was allowed. We apologize we could not accomodate for all plus ones.

Where can I send money?

We have a specific tab for our honeymoon or last minute gift items! Thank you for your gift, we appreciate it greatly!

Where are you registered?

There is a separate tab with all of our registry links. Please purchase through these links so duplicate gifts are avoided!
Please also include gift receipts with gifts in case something is damaged or broken!

Can I bring a gift to the wedding?

While we appreciate your desire to bring a gift, we ask all gifts to be sent directly to us or given during a wedding shower or another time! There will be a card box for cards on the day of the wedding! We appreciate anything given and gifts are not a requirement!

I can't attend but want to celebrate

We understand Tuesday may not work for everyone who is invited and wants to attend. We hope a time can be set up to celebrate with you personally! We also will be having several wedding showers that many guests will be invited to!

There will also be a video recording of the ceremony and moments from the reception that will be shared when available to us!
